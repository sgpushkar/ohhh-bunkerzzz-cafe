# Drop by Bunkerzzz Cafe

Drop by Bunkerzzz Cafe is a moody, beachside cafe website for a sundowner spot near Pirwadi Beach, Nagaon, Uran. The site presents the cafe as a cozy hangout for comfort food, cold drinks, sunset moments, and casual gatherings by the coast.

## Website Overview

The homepage is a single-page cafe experience with smooth section navigation and a dark neon visual style. It highlights the cafe's atmosphere, food menu, gallery, location, customer reviews, and contact details.

## Main Sections

- **Hero**: A full-screen opening section with the cafe mood, primary tagline, and quick links to the menu and directions.
- **About**: A short story about the cafe, its beachside vibe, and key highlights.
- **Menu**: A categorized food and drinks menu with images, prices, and popular item labels.
- **Gallery**: A visual grid showing the cafe space, food, beach moments, and ambience.
- **Location**: A find-us section with an embedded map, address, hours, phone number, and Instagram handle.
- **Reviews**: Customer-style review cards with ratings and short testimonials.
- **Footer**: Brand summary, navigation links, social links, and visit details.

## Visual Style

The website uses a dark cafe-inspired palette with neon cyan and orange accents. Large photography, glass-style panels, soft glow effects, and bold display typography create a warm late-evening cafe feel.

## Built For

This site is designed for visitors who want to quickly understand the cafe's vibe, browse the menu, see the place, and find directions before visiting.
